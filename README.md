# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, salary distribution, and departmental performance.  
This dashboard helps HR professionals identify trends, improve employee retention strategies, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🚀 Features
- Employee count overview
- Attrition rate analysis
- Average monthly income tracking
- Department-wise attrition insights
- Salary slab distribution
- Age group analysis
- Education field breakdown
- Years at company analysis
- Job role attrition comparison
- Gender filter for dynamic reporting

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Highest attrition occurs in the **Research & Development** department.
- Employees aged **26–35** have the highest attrition count.
- Lower salary slabs experience higher employee turnover.
- Attrition decreases as years at the company increase.
- Certain technical and healthcare-related job roles show higher attrition trends.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI**

- **Data Cleaning & Transformation**
- **Data Visualization**
---

## 🎯 Business Objective
This project aims to:
- Monitor employee attrition trends
- Identify high-risk employee segments
- Support HR decision-making
- Improve workforce retention strategies
